Giin is the loyalty and engagement platform for bars and restaurants in Bangkok. Patrons scan a QR at their table, complete tasks like leaving a review or making a match prediction, and earn rewards from your venue.

How it works

Up and running in an afternoon.

📱

Patrons scan

A QR sticker at every table. Patrons scan with their phone — no app download required.

⚡

Patrons engage

Tasks like Google reviews, social posts, or match predictions. You set the reward.

✅

Staff validate, patrons redeem

Your team scans patron QRs to validate completions and redeem rewards. All in one place.

Why Giin

Works for everyone at the table.

For owners

Drive Google reviews, social proof, and repeat visits. Track engagement per patron, per task, per match — so you know exactly what's working and what isn't.

For patrons

Earn drinks, food, and discounts for things they'd do anyway. One QR, instant rewards. No app to download, no points card to lose.

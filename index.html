How it works

From scan to return visit.

What actually happens, start to finish.

01

Customers scan

A QR code at the table takes them to your tasks — no app, no download. Just a phone number and a quick verification code to start.
02

They complete a task

They do what you've set — spend a little more, post about their visit, try a featured item. Your staff confirms it in a tap, and the reward drops into the customer's Giin profile.
03

They come back to redeem

The reward unlocks during the redemption window you set. After the customer redeems, the reward is automatically marked as used, and you've earned a customer visit you might not have had otherwise.

Game predictions

Turn match day into two visits, not one.

Customers predict match results from their phones while they watch. Set the reward to unlock right after the final whistle, and you've got a reason for them to choose your venue for the game — and stay through to the end. Set it to unlock on a later visit, and the same prediction brings them back another day. One feature, working both the match and the return.

Getting started

Set up in an afternoon. Running forever.

No new hardware, no POS integration, no IT project. We handle setup with you in about half an hour — then your only job is handing customers a reason to come back.

1

One quick meeting or call

We learn your venue and set up your first tasks and rewards together — and show your admin how to do it, so you can create and change tasks yourself anytime. About thirty minutes, start to finish.
2

We bring your QR codes

In our setup meeting we'll plan where your QR codes go and design the signage they sit on — table tents, counter cards, wall posters, whatever suits your space. Then we print everything and bring it to you, ready to place.
3

Your whole team is ready

Add your staff to your Giin team and they get the Giin staff scanner to validate tasks and redeem rewards during service. They sign in and use it right from their own phones — no shared device, no extra hardware.

A short training video and a quick in-person session get your whole team comfortable — and you're never stuck, with a direct line to us for support whenever you need it.

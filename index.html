What you get

Giin works in two ways.

Every customer who walks in becomes worth more — on the visit they're already making, and on the visits you wouldn't otherwise have gotten.

1
More from today's visit

Giin gets your customers to do things that benefit you while they're still in the room.

Get more revenue per visit

Set tasks that nudge customers to spend a little more while they're with you — a bigger round, one more dish, a featured item.

Turn customers into promotion

Reward customers for posting about you or leaving a review, and let every visit feed your social presence and reputation.

2
More visits you wouldn't have had

Turn one visit into the next

Rewards earned on one visit are redeemed on a later one, giving customers a built-in reason to return, not just visit once.

Fill your slow days

Set rewards to redeem only on slow days or off-peak hours, and turn your deadest shifts into profitable ones.

Getting started

Set up in an afternoon. Running forever.

No new hardware, no POS integration, no IT project. We handle setup with you in about half an hour — then your only job is handing customers a reason to come back.

1

One quick meeting or call

We learn your venue and set up your first tasks and rewards together — and show your admin how to do it, so you can create and change tasks yourself anytime. About thirty minutes, start to finish.
2

We bring your QR codes

In our setup meeting we'll plan where your QR codes go and design the signage they sit on — table tents, counter cards, wall posters, whatever suits your space. Then we print everything and bring it to you, ready to place.
3

Your whole team is ready

Add your staff to your Giin team and they get the Giin staff scanner to validate tasks and redeem rewards during service. They sign in and use it right from their own phones — no shared device, no extra hardware.

A short training video and a quick in-person session get your whole team comfortable — and you're never stuck, with a direct line to us for support whenever you need it.
